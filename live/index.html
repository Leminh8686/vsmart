BEAUTIFULLY MADE
Live is synonymous with elegance. The screen extends to the edge of the phone, which is reduced to a bare minimum, and the colour mix creates a three-dimensional effect that blends into the frame. The result: a design for people who want to be noticed.
Triple camera,
professional results
Discover how far you can go with Live’s triple camera: The first one captures impressive images thanks to its 48MP. The second creates a depth map that generates the bokeh effect, blurring the background and highlighting the faces with spectacular results, even at night. The third captures panoramic images that you will want to frame, thanks to its super wide-angle lens. Three cameras for a professional result.
Reinventing
the night mode
Get incredible pictures in any environment, even at night. The main camera combines a f/1.7 aperture with the pixel binning technology and the pixel size of 1.6 um, managing to capture sharp images with a high level of detail. Super Night mode technology achieves the best results after dark: capture and merge several snapshots automatically, to conquer the night.
Impressive panoramics
that defy your eyes
Live’s third camera includes a super wide-angle lens, with a viewing angle of 120º. You will capture incredible images, spectacular landscapes and impressive perspectives without missing any detail.
Flawless selfies
in full splendour
Achieve the perfect selfie with a front camera that breaks with conventions: 20MP, AI to achieve the bokeh effect with a single camera and integrated modes like Face Beauty with Body Reshape to always capture the best version of you.
NORMAL MODE BEAUTY MODE
Bigger, livelier
Enjoy an extraordinary audiovisual experience on your 6.2″ FHD+ AMOLED Infinity Display. Ultra-vivid colours, contrast brought to the maximum exponent and the most impressive black tones you’ve ever seen. An ultra-sharp and energy-efficient screen with minimum battery consumption.
The world
at your fingertips
Unlock your smartphone with ease thanks to the built-in fingerprint sensor on the screen, giving you access in a flash.
Enduring power
at the core
More power and more efficiency. The Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 675 octa-core processor up to 2.0 GHz offers great performance even in the most demanding conditions, with higher efficiency and lower energy consumption thanks to the exclusive Kyro™ technology.
Long live the battery
4000mAh of battery lasts and lasts. Specifically, for more than 19 hours of navigation, 15 hours of streaming, 6 hours of gaming or 63 hours of music playback. And with Qualcomm® Quick Charge™ 3.0, you can go from 0 to 50% battery in just over 30 minutes.
VOS: more advanced,
more intuitive
VOS combines the simplicity of Android with extra features that make the interface more comfortable and intuitive. Choose how to navigate your mobile and interact with your apps more smoothly. “Simple mode” minimalises the interface even more and allows you to add contacts directly on the home screen.
